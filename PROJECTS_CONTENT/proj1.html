une_impression_short_film

"une impression' is a short film, directed, shot and edited by myself. Made in collaboration with Paul Ruche, who's responsible for the original idea (and is also the actor). We developed this project from april to december 2024.

pre_production

- Wrote the script in collaboration with Paul Ruche

- Recorded and edited a demo version of the short film, to have a better grip on the visual part of the project

- Made the pertinent changes to the script to get a more appealing project, correcting the mistakes made in the demo version
setting up the lights

production_and_shooting

- Managed the transport logistics of all the material

- Recorded the whole short film alone

- Lighted the whole short film alone

- Acted as photography director

post_production

- Selected and organized the material

- Edited the short film

- Matte paintings for shots requiring VFX

- VFX (cloning the subject)

- Color correction

audio_design

- Designed harmonic musical elements

- Recorded and designed foleys and ambiances

- Mixed and mastered the soundtrack
mixer from the project
